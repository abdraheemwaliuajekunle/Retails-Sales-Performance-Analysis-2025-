# Retail Sales Performance Analysis (2025)

## 1. Project Overview
This project involves a comprehensive analysis of a retail company’s transactional data for the fiscal year 2025. The goal was to transform raw logistics and sales records into a high-level executive dashboard to identify growth drivers and operational inefficiencies.

## 2. Project Scope
The analysis evaluates over 8,000 completed transactions across four key geographic regions (West, South, Northeast, and Midwest) and three primary sales channels (Mobile App, Website, and In-Store).

## 3. Project Objectives
* *Inventory Strategy:* Identify high-performing product categories and individual products.
* *Channel Optimization:* Quantify the performance of sales channels to optimize marketing spend.
* *Market Expansion:* Analyze customer segments and geographic distribution to identify opportunities.
* *Seasonal Planning:* Visualize monthly and quarterly sales trends.

## 4. Expected Outcomes
* A centralized, interactive dashboard for executive-level decision-making.
* Identification of the "May Slump" and "September Surge" for proactive revenue management.
* Strategic insights for increasing the Average Order Value (AOV).

## 5. Document Purpose
This document serves as a technical case study detailing the end-to-end data analysis process, from initial data cleaning to final business recommendations.

## 6. Use Case
This analysis is intended for stakeholders to address regional underperformance, specifically in the Midwest, and capitalize on the high conversion rates in the Mobile App channel.

## 7. Data Cleaning and Processing
Using *Microsoft Excel*, the data underwent a rigorous preparation phase:
* *Standardization:* Cleaned inconsistent logistics partner names using TRIM and Text-to-Columns.
* *Technical Troubleshooting:* Resolved syntax errors and addressed Pivot Table discrepancies.
* *Feature Engineering:* Developed calculated columns for *Total Cost, **Profit, and **Profit Margin*.
* Remove unnecessary Columns from the dataset e.g discount, customers_emails.

## 8. Data Analysis
The analysis revealed the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
* *Total Revenue:* $1,266,390
* *Total Profit:* $634,256
* *Profit Margin:* 50%
* *Average Order Value:* $158

## 9. Data Visualization
An interactive *Sales Performance Dashboard* was developed to present findings:
* *Regional Analysis:* West ($434,240) and South ($413,245) were primary contributors.
* *Category Performance:* Electronics ($374,980) was the leading category.
* *Channel Performance:* Mobile App ($632,835) was the dominant channel.

## 10. Business Recommendations
* *Mobile-First Strategy:* Prioritize Mobile App UX as it generates nearly 50% of revenue.
* *Regional Allocation:* Increase marketing spend in the West and South regions.
* *Inventory & Bundling:* Use the Smart Fitness Watch to create bundles that increase AOV.
* *Customer Retention:* Implement a tiered VIP program for top-spending customers.
* *Seasonal Management:* Launch campaigns to bridge the May revenue gap.

## 11. Conclusion
The 2025 Sales Performance Analysis successfully translated raw data into a strategic roadmap. By focusing on high-performing digital channels and dominant regional markets, the business can maintain its 50% profit margin while addressing growth potential in underperforming segments.
